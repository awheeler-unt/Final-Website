In Depth Analysis of DREDGE.

DREDGE is on the surface a simple fishing game, but the game itself, even in its simplist moments, brings up moral questions.

Gameplay

Dredge is an open world fishing game where you collect fish and items to complete quests for individuals, sell items for money, upgrade your boat and discover the mysteries of the world you are in. Through tips from the residents from across the map you are given guide on how to complete certain tasks and missiond to achieve.

The controls of the game on keyboard follow a standard WASD format for movement with the mouse used to turn the camera. There is also "F" key to interact with most items, "Tab" to open storage menu, and "E" to select different ablities. Other hotkeys to streamline interaction are included, but the previously mentioned are the most basic. Overall the controls, are realitivly simple to follow and since most items can be accessed through the "Tab" menu memorizing the hotkeys, although useful is not necessary.

Considering the control as well as the low stakes, the game is quite easy to pick up and beginner friendly.
